Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, processes, and fees. We believe in transparency and are here to provide the clarity you need.

General & Initial Consultation

How do I schedule an initial consultation?

expand_more

You can schedule a consultation by calling our office directly or by filling out the contact form on our website. Our team will get back to you promptly to arrange a convenient time.

What should I bring to my first meeting?

expand_more

Please bring any documents relevant to your case, such as contracts, correspondence, police reports, or court notices. Also, a list of questions you have for the attorney can be very helpful. This ensures our time together is as productive as possible.

Is my consultation confidential?

expand_more

Absolutely. All communications during your consultation are protected by attorney-client privilege, ensuring complete confidentiality, regardless of whether you decide to hire our firm.

Billing & Fees

How does the firm charge for its services?

expand_more

We offer various fee structures, including hourly rates, flat fees, and contingency fees, depending on the nature of the case. We will discuss the most suitable option for you during the initial consultation.

Are there any upfront costs or retainers?

expand_more

For many cases, we require a retainer, which is an advance payment held in a trust account. Legal fees and costs are then deducted from this retainer as they are incurred. We provide detailed monthly invoices for full transparency.

The Legal Process

How long will my case take?

expand_more

The duration of a legal case varies significantly based on its complexity, the court's schedule, and the cooperation of the opposing party. We will provide you with an estimated timeline and keep you informed of any developments.

How will I be kept updated on the progress of my case?

expand_more

We are committed to maintaining open communication. You will receive regular updates from your attorney via phone or email, and we are always available to answer any questions you may have about your case's status.

Have a Different Question?

Our team is ready to provide the answers and guidance you need. Reach out to us for a personalized and confidential consultation.
